 - o
 - u

 - (ae) - ä
 - (oe) - ö
 - (ue) - ü

### Plosive
 - p/b

 - k/g
### Nasal
 - m
 - n
 - ng
### Fricative
 - f/v
 - sh/j
 - s/z
 - ch/r
### Other
 - h/l
The voicing can be changed from unvoiced x/- to voiced -/x , whith exception of the nasal konsonants.
